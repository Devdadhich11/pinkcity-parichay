History of Jaipur

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is the capital of Rajasthan, India. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaipur is known for its historic palaces, forts, and vibrant culture. Discover its fascinating past and rich heritage.

Attractions

Explore the majestic attractions of Jaipur, including the Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, and City Palace. Each site offers a glimpse into the city's architectural brilliance and history.

Food & Cafes

Experience Jaipur's diverse culinary scene, from traditional Rajasthani dishes to modern cafes. Indulge in local delicacies and explore trendy spots across the city.

Events

Stay updated with the latest events happening in Jaipur. From cultural festivals to art exhibitions, find out what's happening in the city and plan your visit accordingly.
